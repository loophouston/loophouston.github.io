Loop Houston

Episode 2: Lennart Koopmann

August 19, 2016

Lennart Koopmann is one of Houston's many imports.

Lennart grew up in the German countryside and moved to Houston via Hamburg in 2015. Lennart moved to Houston to grow the company he founded in Germany, open source log management software company, Graylog. (Don't worry! Lennart explains what the hell that means in the interview. :-) )

Some of the topics we cover in our interview with Lennart:

  • Origin story of Graylog
  • How does an open source software company make money?
  • Business differences between Germany and the U.S.
  • Transitioning from technical founder to CEO
  • Taking public transit to work every day (Yes! In Houston!)
  • Lennart's favorite restaurants and bars in Houston?
